Paella

A dish whose main ingredient is rice, which is cooked with other ingredients such as fish, seafood, poultry, meat, vegetables, pulses, etc. It is a typical dish in all Spanish regions, especially in Valencia, with the type and quantity of ingredients varying in each one.

Ingredients

300g rice for Paella 12 oz. linguine 1/4 c. olive oil
1 red chile, thinly sliced 1/2 c. grated Parmesan cheese Special pane for Paella
