Roasted eggplant burger

Ingredients

  • 4 slices American cheese

    2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp. paprika

    1/2 c. quick-cooking oats

  • 2 tbsp. olive oil

  • 1 large egg yolk

  • 1 medium onion

How To Prepare
